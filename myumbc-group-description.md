## Description (paste into the rich text editor)

**StudyHub** is a free, student-built platform that gives UMBC students a smarter way to study together.

Think of it like GitHub, but for your study materials. You can:

- **Share study sheets** — Upload notes for any course so your classmates can find them
- **Fork & improve** — Take someone's notes, make them better, and contribute your changes back
- **Practice tests** — Quiz yourself before the exam with auto-scored practice tests
- **Course-based browsing** — Find materials organized by UMBC courses, not buried in group chats
- **Private notes** — Keep your own markdown notes linked to your classes
- **Download anything** — Grab study sheets and practice tests to review offline

No paywalls. No ads. No email required to sign up. Just students helping students.

**Website:** https://www.getstudyhub.net
**Discord:** [link to your Discord server]

StudyHub was built by a UMBC student who got tired of losing notes in dead group chats. If you've ever searched for a study guide the night before an exam and found nothing — this is for you.

Join us, share your best notes, and let's make studying at UMBC a little less painful.
